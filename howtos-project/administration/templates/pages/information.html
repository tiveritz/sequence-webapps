How-to

A How To is a step-by-step instruction with the goal of reaching a certain objective. This page lets you edit the entry points in the user application.

Step

A step is part of a How-to. Steps either have a description or linked substeps. These steps can be re-used.

Superstep

A superstep is a step that has one or more steps connected to it. A superstep can be used as substep of another superstep.

Substep

A substep is a step that is used in a superstep. A substep can be a superstep.
